An Unforgettable Anime Event Experience

28 Dec 2024 • Personal Blog

Attending an anime event has always been on my bucket list, and recently, I finally got the chance to experience one. From the moment I stepped into the venue, I was shocked by the length of the queue to get inside. I barely could feel my leg, and the sunlight was burning my skin. Thankfully, I used a skincare and brought an umbrella. But once I made it inside, I was really excited because I had never experienced a big event before.

Cosplay Galore
One of the most exciting aspects of the event was hunting the cosplayers. People went all out, dressing up as their favorite characters with incredible attention to detail. From anime character and game character, to meme character, the variety was astonishing. I even took pictures with some of the cosplayers, who were more than happy to bring my favorite characters to life before my own eyes.

Merchandise Wonderland
The event was a paradise for anime collectors. There were countless booths selling anime merchandise, including posters, keychains, figurines, and even limited-edition items. It's too bad that the merchandise that I wanted was pretty expensive, although I did get some free stickers and a fan.

Live Performances
One of the highlights was attending a live performance by a famous singer. The energy of the crowd was infectious, and I found myself singing along with everyone.

Meeting Famous Content Creator
Another exciting moment was meeting a famous content creator that I’ve followed for a long time. Seeing them in person felt unreal. Unfortunately, I didn't get the chance to take a picture, and I even missed meeting one of my favorite Youtubers, I wish I could go back in time just to take a picture that person.

Final Thoughts
Reflecting on this experience, I realize how much joy and excitement an anime event can bring. Despite a few regrets, like missing a photo opportunity, the event was unforgettable. If you ever get the chance to attend an anime event, go for it! It’s an adventure that will leave you with lasting memories.
